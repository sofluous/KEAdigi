How to Play

This module is for inspection, not scoring. Try changing one thing at a time and watch what happens.

  • Load your own image or use the sample.
  • Zoom in and out to see the same picture at different distances.
  • Drag the image around the viewport to inspect different areas.
  • Use the pixel neighborhood screen to see how nearby colors build the image illusion.
