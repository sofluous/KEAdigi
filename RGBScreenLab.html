How to Play

This module shows how a screen creates color by mixing light.

  • Adjust the red, green, and blue sliders.
  • Watch the subpixel panel to see how each light changes.
  • Look at the blended screen preview to see the combined color.
  • Try the presets to compare common screen colors.
